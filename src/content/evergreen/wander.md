Once upon a time people had ~ blogrolls ~ Well here in 2026, we have wander pages (a la [James’ Coffee Blog](https://jamesg.blog/wander)).

- [A Collection of Unmitigated Pedantry](https://acoup.blog/) by Bret Devereaux: Do you like reading about the Roman Republic? Of course you do. Dr Devereaux is probably the leading public intellectual on the classic Mediterranean, not to mention fantasy worldbuilding; his _very_ long posts are scholarly but eminently readable.
- [anhvn](https://anhvn.com/): Anh is a designer (?) based in Vancouver (??) I think (???). But in any case she redesigns her site about once a month and it’s always downright delightful.
- [Robin Sloan](https://www.robinsloan.com/): C’mon if you’ve spent ten minutes on this site you know I read every word the man writes.
